01

Brand Personality

The core traits that define who we are

Empowering

We believe every woman deserves to feel confident in her style. Pretty Paid Closet isn't just about clothes—it's about helping women own their look and know their worth.

Curated

Not a thrift store. Not fast fashion. Every piece is hand-selected with intention. We bring an experienced eye to sourcing—quality over quantity, always.

Accessible

Premium style shouldn't require a premium budget. We make elevated fashion attainable for style-forward women who shop smart, not just cheap.

02

Tone of Voice

How we speak to our community

Confident, Not Cocky

We speak with assurance about our curation and style expertise. We celebrate bold choices without being arrogant. There's a difference between knowing your worth and being boastful.

Warm, Not Casual

Our voice is approachable and friendly—like shopping with your stylish friend who always knows where to find the best pieces. But we maintain polish and professionalism.

Empowering, Not Preachy

We lift women up without lecturing them. Our messaging inspires confidence and self-worth naturally, through the way we present our brand and products.

Real, Not Performative

Authenticity over trends. We're not chasing what's viral—we're building a brand with substance. Our voice reflects genuine expertise and real connection.

04

Language Guidelines

Words we use and words we avoid

We Say

  • Curated, hand-selected, intentional
  • Confident, empowered, elevated
  • Smart shopping, style-forward
  • Premium quality, designer-inspired
  • "She knows her worth"
  • "Elegance, earned"
  • Investment pieces, wardrobe staples
  • Clean lines, flattering fit

We Don't Say

  • Cheap, bargain, discount bin
  • Thrifted, secondhand, used
  • OMG, slay queen, sis (casual slang)
  • Fast fashion, trendy, viral
  • Limited time only! Act now!
  • Excessive emojis or exclamation marks
  • Price-focused messaging as primary hook
  • Desperation or urgency tactics

Platform Guidelines

Consistency across every touchpoint

Poshmark

  • Profile banner uses brand colors and logo
  • Closet descriptions reflect brand voice
  • Listing titles: Clean, descriptive (no ALL CAPS)
  • Photos: Bright, well-lit, consistent backgrounds
  • Comments: Warm but professional responses

Instagram

  • Feed aesthetic: Cohesive color palette
  • Stories: Behind-the-scenes, new arrivals
  • Reels: Styling tips, outfit inspiration
  • Captions: Brand voice, no excessive hashtags
  • Bio: Tagline + clear value proposition

Website

  • Homepage: Brand story, curated selections
  • Product pages: Detailed descriptions in brand voice
  • About: Jazz's story, brand values
  • Checkout: Clean, trustworthy, gold accents
  • Email capture: Value-driven, not discount-focused

Email Marketing

  • Welcome email: Warm introduction, brand story
  • New arrivals: Curated picks, styled inspiration
  • Abandoned cart: Encouraging, not desperate
  • Subject lines: Intriguing, confident tone
  • Design: Minimal, gold accents, clear CTAs

09

Photography Style

Visual standards for all imagery

Product Photography

  • Bright, natural lighting preferred
  • Clean backgrounds (white, cream, soft pink)
  • Show garment details and quality
  • Flat lays styled with intention
  • On-body shots where possible
  • Consistent editing style across all images

Lifestyle & Brand

  • Diverse representation of women
  • Confident, natural poses
  • Real moments, not overly staged
  • Warm color grading, soft contrast
  • Settings that reflect the customer lifestyle
  • Behind-the-scenes of curation process
